Overview

What if SAP applications were simple and easy to use? Now that’s a reality!

Introducing SAP Fiori - A collection of apps with
a simple and easy to use experience for broadly
and frequently used SAP software functions that work
seamlessly across devices – desktop,
tablet, or smartphone.

Keeping Simple
Things Simple

Imagine being able to quickly enter your time, check your sales orders, approve requests and more from anywhere. The first release of SAP Fiori includes 25 apps for the most common functions for workflow approvals, information lookups and self-service tasks.

Leverage SAP Investment

SAP Fiori enables you to leverage your existing SAP investments by delivering powerful business results. You can get started with SAP Fiori immediately and bring instant value to all your employees.
